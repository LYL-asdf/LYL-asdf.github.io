Research & Expertise

My research primarily focuses on developing AI-based surrogate models and digital twin systems for cardiac electrophysiology and mechanics.

By combining deep learning architectures (such as GCN, LSTM, and Mamba) with high-performance computing, I explore the application of multi-task learning in predicting myocardial infarction scars and their transmurality, as well as solving cardiac inverse problems.
